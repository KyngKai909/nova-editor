Why Nova

AI builders lock you in. IDEs make you type.
Nova does neither.

Most tools force a trade: a prompt box that hides your code, or an editor with no canvas. Nova gives you the canvas, the code, the live app, and AI — over files that never stop being yours.

Edit anything, not just React

HTML, JSX, TSX and Tailwind — any framework, any repo, even a single pasted component. Not locked to one stack.

Run the real app, live

Boot the entire project in the browser and edit it as it actually runs — not a faked preview of a static mock.

Your code, your Git, your keys

Local-first and fully open source. Files live on your disk, changes go through your Git, AI runs on your device or your own key. No backend, no lock-in.

Free AI, on your device

Nova Lite runs a coding model right in your browser — free, private, no key. Want more power? Bring your own key, or upgrade to a managed Nova model. Either way it edits your real files and the canvas updates instantly.

Visual edits stay free

Direct manipulation never burns a token. Use AI when you want leverage, your mouse when you don't — you choose the cost.

One tab, the whole loop

Design, code, run, and commit in a single place. No export step, no handoff, no context-switch between tools.

How it works

From import to pull request, in your browser.

01

Import

Clone a full GitHub repo, open a folder from disk, or paste a component. Nova works in your browser — no upload, no account.

02

Edit visually — or with AI

Click any element and shape real CSS with a full inspector, or ask the built-in AI to make the change. Edits round-trip to source.

03

Run it, then ship

Boot the whole app live in the browser, review a clean diff, then commit, push, or open a pull request — without leaving the tab.

A real editor, not a toy.

Webflow-grade controls over live code. Layout, spacing, typography, color, effects — with a layer tree, breakpoints, preview, and a built-in code editor.
